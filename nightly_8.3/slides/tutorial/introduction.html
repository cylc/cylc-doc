What Is Cylc?§

What Is Cylc?§

Cylc can automatically:

  • Submit tasks across computer systems and resource managers.
  • Recover from failures.
  • Repeat workflows.

What Is Cylc?§

  • Originally developed at NIWA (New Zealand)
  • Now developed by an international partnership including the Met Office (UK).
  • General purpose tool as applicable in business as in scientific research.
